Project Site Plan

Site Name

Temple Explorer

This name represents a platform dedicated to exploring and learning about temples, their histories, and significance across different cultures.

Optional domain availability: templeexplorer.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide comprehensive information on various temples, including their locations, history, and cultural significance. It will also feature a gallery of images and user submissions to foster community engagement.

Scenarios

  • What temples can I visit in my area?
  • What are the opening hours and entry requirements for a specific temple?

Color Schema

Secondary Color: #F0E68C (used for background)

Typography
